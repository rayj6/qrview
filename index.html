3D models in AR via QR code

Scan with your phone’s camera → tap the link → view the model in your space. No extra app.

How it works

  1. Open your smartphone’s default camera app.
  2. Point it at the QR code.
  3. Tap the link that appears.
  4. View the 3D model in WebAR (or use “View in your space” for full AR).

Works on modern iOS and Android, and on Apple Vision Pro.

Use cases

  • AR previews: Let customers view products (furniture, sports gear) in their space.
  • Share 3D scans: Host your scan as .glb, create a QR, and share the link.
  • Product info: Link from packaging or print to an interactive 3D model.
  • 3D printed QR codes: Use tools like QRCode2STL or MakerWorld to make physical QR codes that open 3D/AR content.
